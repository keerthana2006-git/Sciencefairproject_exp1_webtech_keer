🌍 About Science Fair

The Science Fair Project Site provides students with innovative science project ideas and encourages them to develop creative thinking, problem-solving abilities, and presentation skills.

Science fairs help students apply scientific concepts in real-life situations through experiments and working models.

📖 Scientific Terms

Hypothesis
An educated prediction before conducting an experiment.

Observation
Collecting and recording information carefully.

Conclusion
The final result obtained after the experiment.

  2. Research the Topic
